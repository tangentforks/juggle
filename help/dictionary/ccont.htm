continue.

continue. may be used within a for. , while. , or whilst. control structure, and goes to the top of the innermost such enclosing structure. See also break. .
 

For example:
sumeven=: 3 : 0
 s=.0
 for_j. i.y. do.
  if. 2|j do. continue. end.
  s=.j+s
 end.
)

   sumeven 9
20

   +/ (* -.@(2&|)) i.9
20

   sumeven 1000
249500

   +/ (* -.@(2&|)) i.1000
249500

   (sumeven = 2&!@>.&.-:) 1000
1
